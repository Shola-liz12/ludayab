About Us

Luday AB is an Information technology company driven by a passion to give to socciety and make the world a better place. The company was established in year 2005.

The company thrives in innovative ideas and solution to everyday challenges. It also help other companies and individuals to actualize their dreams through realistic and practical website and Appliation designs.

The company started in Sweden by two siblings, has now become a global company, with its users scattered across the globe.

We specializes in digital marketing, we are in the business of making your business stand out from the crowd using the power of today's technology.
